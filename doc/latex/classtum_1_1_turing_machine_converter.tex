\subsection{\-Detailed \-Description}
\-Class for converting a given tape to an encoded tape. 

\-The original tape is the tape from an arbitrary \-Turing \-Machine. \-There are 4 encoded tapes\-: \hyperlink{classtum_1_1_tape}{\-Tape} 1\-: \-The simulation tape. \hyperlink{classtum_1_1_tape}{\-Tape} 2\-: \-The encodation of the original \-T\-M. \hyperlink{classtum_1_1_tape}{\-Tape} 3\-: \-The current state. \hyperlink{classtum_1_1_tape}{\-Tape} 4\-: \-The working tape (scrap tape).
